## Session 2: Predicting ADMET properties for your compounds of interest

ADMET properties are a fundamental consideration in drug discovery pipelines. ADMET refers to Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, Excretion, and Toxicity. These properties determine the pharmacokinetic and safety profiles of drug candidates and play a critical role in predicting how a compound behaves in the human body.&#x20;
